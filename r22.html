Dim Bhapa


30 mins
2 serving
Intermediate

DESCRIPTION

This is an excellent easy and quick dish for people who do not eat meat but eat eggs.

INGREDIENTS

4 pc eggs
3 tbsp white mustard seeds or 1.5 tbsp black mustard seeds
3 tbsp poppy seeds
1 tbsp grated coconut or coconut powder (optional)
1 tbsp mustard oil
1 tsp turmeric powder
3 pc green chilies
1 tbsp sugar
salt to taste
water

DIRECTIONS

Preparing the Dim Bhapa Recipe Marinade
1.Soak the mustard and poppy seeds in water for 20 minutes.
2.In a mixer-grinder, prepare a fine paste of the soaked mustard seeds and poppy seeds.
3.Boil the eggs for 6-8 minutes on high flame.You will need hard boiled eggs.
4.Take a tiffin box and pour the mustard seeds and poppy seeds paste.
5.Add the turmeric, slit chilies, salt, sugar, mustard oil and coconut powder.
6.Give it a nice whisk with a spoon to mix all the ingredients uniformly.
7.Add the eggs in the tiffin box after de-shelling them.
8.Take spoonfuls of the marinade and pour over the eggs.
9.Close the lid of the tiffin box.
Cooking the Dim Bhapa Recipe
10.In a sauce pan or pressure cooker, put in the tiffin box.
11.Add water so that the level of the water does not rise more than 40% of the tiffin box.
12.Cover the sauce pan or pressure cooker to prevent the steam from escaping.
13.Switch on the flame and let the steam cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
14.Once done, switch off the flame and very carefully take the tiffin box out of the sauce pan or pressure cooker. Remember that it will be very hot and hence you should exercise extreme caution during this step.
