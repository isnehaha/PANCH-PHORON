Gondhoraj lebur Shorbot!!


20 mins
4 serving
Intermediate

DESCRIPTION

Every Bengali kid of our generation spending their summer vacations at their grandparents place would vividly remember the heavenly taste and enthusiasm of the most simple drink, Gandharaj Lebu-r Sharbat.

INGREDIENTS

Gandharaj Lebu – 1
Chilled water – 4 glass
Sugar – 5 tbspn
Salt – 1tspn

DIRECTIONS

1. Cut the lemon in half and squeeze the lemon to extract juice. Scratch the skin a bit to extract the citrus scent since Gandharaj Lebu has very less juice.
2. Mix and stir all the ingredient till the sugar dissolves.
3.Strain the lemonade to remove pulps
Lemon Drink.jpg Serve and enjoy!!
